Why You Should Eat Healthy

  1. To Survive
  2. To have more energy
  3. To avoid disease and illness
  4. To keep medical costs down
  5. To live a longer life for children and grandchildren
  6. To feel empowered and confident
  7. To look better
  8. For better mental health

There are so many benefits to being healthy, however many people can’t feel them because they are using quick fixes to make them feel better. Healthy living makes you feel good, motivated and present in the moment. When these things start coming together one can start truly enjoying life, everyone in it and everything they do. To feel this satisfaction our body needs to work optimally. When we eat healthy, we nourish our body to work efficiently and effectively. That doesn’t mean we can’t have a treat or two, our bodies can digest brownies and ice cream, they just can’t operate off them. This website will explore all the nutrients the body needs.
Click on each nutrient for detailed explanations of what they are, what they do, and why we need them.
